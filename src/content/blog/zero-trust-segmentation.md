Campus networks are collaborative by design, which makes segmentation harder to implement without friction. This piece documents a staged approach using policy tiers, identity-based access, and a feedback loop with faculty and lab managers.

## Implementation highlights

- Start with high-risk services and expand gradually.
- Combine identity policy with device posture.
- Publish clear escalation paths for research exceptions.

## Result

The network gains stronger isolation while preserving the agility students and researchers need.
